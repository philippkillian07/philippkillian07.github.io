Computer Science Student @ ELTE University

I am a Computer Science student at Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE) in Budapest, having recently completed an international exchange term at the Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH) in South Korea. With an international background spanning Germany, Hungary, and the United States, I bring a global perspective to technical problem-solving.

My current interests have shifted toward concurrent systems and robust software design. In my spare time I enjoy staying active through skiing and swimming.
